Discreet Disguise

Our check-in system is disguised as a standard hydration reminder app, allowing you to verify your safety without alerting your date.

  • Appears as innocent water reminder notification
  • Custom scheduling (30min, 1hr, 2hr intervals)
  • Response options look completely normal
  • No indication it's a safety app

Smart Location Sharing

If you miss a check-in or signal distress, the app automatically begins sharing your location with emergency contacts.

  • Precise GPS location tracking
  • Updates in real-time as you move
  • Shares route history for the past 30 minutes
  • Battery-efficient background tracking

Emergency Protocol

Our comprehensive emergency response system activates silently when you signal distress or miss a scheduled check-in.

  • Silent notifications to emergency contacts
  • Automatic audio recording for evidence
  • Option to escalate to emergency services
  • AI analysis of situation severity
